About UPSKILL

Empowering creativity through online learning

Our Mission

UPSKILL is dedicated to making extracurricular and artistic education accessible to everyone. We believe that learning creative skills should be easy, enjoyable, and available to all, regardless of location or schedule.

What We Offer

Our platform connects passionate instructors with eager learners across a wide range of disciplines including Mehendi Design, Aari Work, Bridal Makeup, Hair Styling, Baking, Crochet, Painting, Handicrafts, Calligraphy, Jewelry Making, and more.

For Students

Browse through our extensive course library, enroll in classes that interest you, and learn at your own pace through high-quality video lessons. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, we have courses for every level.

For Instructors

Share your expertise with students worldwide. Create courses, upload lessons, and even sell your handcrafted products through our marketplace. Build your brand and grow your business while doing what you love.

Artisan Marketplace

Our unique marketplace feature allows instructors to showcase and sell their handcrafted products directly to students and art enthusiasts. Connect with talented artisans and purchase authentic, handmade items.

Get Started Today

Join our growing community of creative learners and instructors. Whether you want to learn a new skill or share your expertise, UPSKILL is here to support your journey.
